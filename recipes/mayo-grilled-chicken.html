Mayo Grilled Chicken

Description

This is a low calorie grilled chicken breast that is perfect for a high protein meal on a calorie restricted diet. The use of mayonnaise instead of traditional oil enhances the flavour and browning of the chicken.

Ingredients

  • 300-350g of chicken breast
  • 2-3 tablespoons of full fat mayonnaise
  • 1-2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1-2 teaspoons of other seasoning of choice
  • Non-stick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Slice chicken breasts in half.
  2. Add salt and seasonings and rub into chicken.
  3. Place mayonnaise and chicken into a bowl and cover on all sides.
  4. Heat grill pan at high-medium heat and apply non-stick cooking spray.
  5. Gently place chicken breasts onto the grill pan for 4-5 minutes or until brown.
  6. Flip chicken breasts onto uncooked side and leave for 4-5 minutes or until completely cooked through.
  7. Either serve whole or slice the chicken breasts into strips.
